Frequently Asked Questions

What is my Riot ID?

Your Riot ID is a unique username that you use to login, like AryanBa3a, and not your display name, like Ba3a#5807.

Why do you need my password?

We need your password in order to fetch your store data from Riot Games. Please note that sharing just your password does not endanger your account. Learn more here.

Can I check my store without entering password?

Yes, we have designed our system such that you can still use our services without sharing your password with us. Learn more about Passwordless login here.

Where do I get help?

When does the Valorant store reset?

The in-game Valorant store resets every 24 hours at 1:00 am UTC / 8:00 pm EST / 6:30 am IST. The store is randomly generated for each Valorant player.

Can I purchase weapon skins from this website?

No, you can not purchase weapon skins direcly from this website. However, we show everything else about the skins that you get in store, like cost (VP) and finisher video.
